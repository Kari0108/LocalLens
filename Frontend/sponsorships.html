Frequently Asked Questions

What metrics do brands look for?

Most Indian brands value engagement over follower count. They look for authentic creators with dedicated audiences interested in travel across India's diverse regions.

How long do sponsorships typically last?

Initial collaborations range from single posts to 3-month partnerships. Successful relationships often extend to year-long ambassador programs.

Do I need to visit specific locations?

Some brands target specific Indian destinations like Rann of Kutch, Kerala backwaters, or Coorg hills, while others focus on your content style regardless of location.
