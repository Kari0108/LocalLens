Description

Join us for an unforgettable culinary adventure through the historic lanes of Old Delhi. This guided food walk takes you to 6-7 authentic eateries that have been serving delicious food for generations.

You'll get to taste a variety of dishes including:

  • Famous Dahi Bhalla
  • Jalebi and Rabri
  • Authentic Mughlai curries
  • Old Delhi's special Kebabs
  • Traditional sweets and desserts

Our local food expert will share fascinating stories about the history and culture of this gastronomic paradise.
