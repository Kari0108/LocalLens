About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable culinary adventure through the historic streets of Old Delhi. This guided food walk will take you through the bustling lanes of Chandni Chowk, where centuries-old recipes are still prepared using traditional methods.

Explore the vibrant food scene of Old Delhi with our expert local guide who knows all the hidden gems and legendary eateries. From mouth-watering chaat and kebabs to decadent sweets, you'll taste authentic flavors that have made Delhi a food lover's paradise.

During this 3-hour walk, we'll visit 6-7 carefully selected food stops, each with its own unique specialty and story. You'll learn about the history and cultural significance of each dish while savoring the best street food Delhi has to offer.

What to Expect

• Tastings at 6-7 famous eateries and street food vendors
