Our Story

Local Lens began in 2018 as a small collective of photographers with a shared passion for capturing the diverse beauty of India. What started as weekend photography meetups in Delhi soon evolved into a movement to document and share authentic Indian experiences with the world.

Founded by passionate students of BTECH CE, our initial team set out on a journey across North India, capturing everything from bustling urban streets to serene rural landscapes. Our photographs quickly gained attention on social media, and we began receiving requests from travelers who wanted to experience the places we had documented.

Today, we've grown into a nationwide community of photographers, guides, and cultural ambassadors united by our love for India's rich heritage and diverse landscapes. Our mission remains the same: to showcase the authentic beauty of India through a local lens.

Our Values

Our Values

At Local Lens, we're guided by core principles that shape everything we do. These values reflect our commitment to authentic storytelling and responsible tourism.

Authenticity

We value true representation of Indian culture, places, and people. We never stage our photographs or create artificial experiences.

Cultural Respect

We approach each community with deep respect for local customs, traditions, and knowledge. We learn before we share.

Sustainability

We promote responsible travel and ensure our operations benefit local communities while minimizing environmental impact.

Community

We believe in collaborative storytelling and creating meaningful connections between travelers and locals.

Artistic Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in our visual storytelling, always seeking to capture the essence of each moment.

Passion

We're driven by our love for India's diverse cultures and landscapes, and we share this enthusiasm in every experience.
