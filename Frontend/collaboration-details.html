Luxury Resort Content Creation in Bali

Create stunning visual content showcasing our beachfront property and luxury amenities. We're looking for photographers and videographers who can capture the essence of our tropical paradise.

Limited Time Opportunity

This collaboration is accepting applications for the next 14 days. Apply soon to secure your spot!

Opportunity Overview

Coastal Paradise Resort is seeking content creators with a strong portfolio in travel photography and videography to produce high-quality visual content for our marketing campaigns. This collaboration includes a 5-night stay at our luxury beachfront resort in Bali, Indonesia, plus additional compensation for deliverables.

We're looking for creators who can capture the unique atmosphere of our property, from our infinity pools overlooking the ocean to our award-winning spa facilities and culinary experiences.

What You'll Receive

  • 5-night complimentary stay in a Premium Ocean View Suite (value: $2,500)
  • Daily breakfast and dinner for two at our beachfront restaurant
  • Complimentary spa treatment for two
  • Access to all resort facilities including infinity pools, fitness center, and water sports
  • Guided tour of local cultural attractions
  • $1,500 USD compensation for deliverables
  • Airport transfers included

Content Requirements

We're looking for the following deliverables:

  • 15 high-resolution photographs of the property, including rooms, facilities, and experiences
  • 2-3 minute promotional video showcasing the resort experience
  • 5 Instagram posts during your stay
  • 3 Instagram Stories highlighting different aspects of the resort
  • 1 blog post about your experience (min. 800 words)

All content will be used across our marketing channels with proper credit. Exclusive usage rights for the agreed deliverables will be provided to Coastal Paradise Resort.

Creator Requirements

  • Minimum 10K followers on Instagram
  • Engagement rate of at least 3%
  • Professional equipment for high-quality photography/videography
  • Portfolio demonstrating experience in luxury travel content
  • Availability during June-August 2025
