Kerala Sadya Masterclass

Learn the art of preparing Kerala's traditional multi-course feast

Experience Overview

Immerse yourself in the culinary heritage of Kerala with this hands-on masterclass focused on Sadya - the elaborate traditional feast served on banana leaves. Led by a local chef from a traditional Kerala family, this experience takes you through the preparation of multiple dishes that balance the six tastes in Ayurvedic tradition.

You'll learn the techniques for grinding spices, extracting coconut milk, and cooking with traditional ingredients like curry leaves, coconut oil, and kokum. The workshop culminates in a communal feast where you'll enjoy the fruits of your labor the authentic way - seated on the floor, eating with your hands from a fresh banana leaf.

  • Duration: 5-6 hours
  • Group Size: Maximum 6 people
  • Includes: All ingredients, recipes to take home, full Sadya meal
  • Location: Traditional home kitchen in Fort Kochi
  • Language: English (Malayalam translation available)

Dishes You'll Learn to Prepare

Sambar

The signature lentil and vegetable stew flavored with tamarind and a special spice blend.

Avial

Mixed vegetables in a coconut and yogurt sauce with curry leaves and coconut oil.

Thoran

Stir-fried vegetables with grated coconut, mustard seeds, and curry leaves.

Olan

White pumpkin and beans cooked in a light coconut milk gravy.

Pachadi

A yogurt-based side dish with vegetables and coconut, similar to raita.

Payasam

Traditional Kerala sweet dessert made with rice, milk, and jaggery.
