Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform

How do I create an account?
Creating an account is simple! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. You can register using your email, or sign up with your Google or Facebook accounts. Once you've verified your email address, you'll be able to set up your profile and start connecting with our community.
What types of collaborations can I find on the platform?
Our platform offers various collaboration opportunities including: content creation partnerships with hotels and tour companies, joint blogging ventures, photography assignments, guest posting, local guide partnerships, and sponsored trips. The types of collaborations are constantly expanding as our community grows!
Is there a fee to join the travel community?
Basic membership is completely free and gives you access to community discussions, events, and basic networking features. We also offer a Premium tier with additional benefits including enhanced profile visibility, advanced search filters, and direct messaging to businesses and influencers. You can compare plan features on our Pricing page.
How can local businesses join the platform?
Local businesses can register for a Business account that includes features specifically designed for tourism enterprises. After creating your account, you'll need to complete a verification process to confirm your business details. Once verified, you can create your business profile, list events, and connect with content creators for potential collaborations.
How do you verify members of the community?
We take trust seriously in our community. All members go through a basic verification process that confirms their email and identity. For content creators and local experts who want to be featured or offer services, we have an additional verification process that may include reviewing their portfolio, checking references, and confirming their qualifications in specific areas.
