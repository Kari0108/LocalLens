Old Delhi Food Walk

Explore the legendary street food of Chandni Chowk with local foodies who know every spice-laden corner.

Experience Overview

Join a guided culinary journey through Old Delhi's bustling alleys, tasting iconic dishes like parathas at Paranthe Wali Gali, spicy chole bhature, creamy kulfi, piping hot jalebis, and of course, the famous Dilli-style butter chicken. Your guide is a seasoned local foodie who will also share cultural anecdotes, market history, and tips for navigating the maze-like lanes of Chandni Chowk.

What You'll Explore

  • Street stalls that have served generations
  • Heritage sweet shops and paratha kitchens
  • Food culture of Shahjahanabad (Old Delhi)
  • Spices, aromas, and stories behind each dish
