Frequently Asked Questions

What metrics do brands look for in applicants?

Most brands prioritize engagement rate, content quality, and audience demographics relevant to their target market in India. Follower count is considered but isn't the only criterion.

How quickly will I hear back after applying?

Most brands review applications within 7-10 days. If selected, you'll be contacted directly to discuss campaign details, logistics, and contracts.

Do campaigns provide accommodation and travel?

Most campaigns in remote locations like Ladakh, Andamans, or Northeast India include accommodation. Urban campaigns in Delhi, Mumbai, or Bangalore typically offer compensation but may not cover stays.
